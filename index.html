KVRX FEST

KVRX Fest was created with the idea that the music scene should be safe, diverse, inclusive, and joyful. The Fest was created entirely by student DJs at KVRX and we hope you enjoy it.


KVRX UT Austin's student-run radio station. KVRX only plays none of the hits, all of the time, bringing Austin some of the best underground music ya'll didn't know you'd love.

KVRX shares our frequency 91.7FM with KOOP, another great community station, and we're on the air every night. Or tune into us anytime on your HD radio or online. Listen and find some new music!
