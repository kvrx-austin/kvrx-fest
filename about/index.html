ABOUT

KVRX Fest was created with the idea that the music scene should be safe, diverse, inclusive, and joyful.

About KVRX

We are UT Austin's student-run radio station. We only play "none of the hits, all of the time," bringing listeners some of the best music they didn't know they loved. KVRX has live DJs in the booth nearly 24-7, all curating their own programming while focusing on genre diversity and local artists.


KVRX shares our frequency 91.7FM with KOOP, another great community station, and we're on the air every night. Or tune into us anytime on your HD radio or online. Listen and find some new music!
